Frequently asked questions

What services does Ara Discover Technologies offer?

We provide a wide range of services including UI/UX Design, Web & Mobile App Development, Graphic Design, Branding, and Digital Marketing. We also offer tailored software products for education, healthcare, and business management.

Where is Ara Discover Technologies located?

Our headquarters is in Thanjavur, Tamil Nadu, India. However, we proudly serve clients across the globe.

Do you work with international clients?

Absolutely! We work with businesses worldwide and have successfully delivered projects across various countries.

What industries do you specialize in?

We specialize in the education, healthcare, salon/spa, and small business sectors. However, we're open to projects across all industries.

Do you provide both web and mobile app development?

Yes, we design and develop both responsive websites and mobile applications (iOS & Android) tailored to your business needs.

Can I hire you for just design or development only?

Yes! We offer standalone services for UI/UX design, development, branding, or marketing — whatever suits your current needs.

What is your design process like?

Our design process includes research, wireframing, prototyping, UI design, user testing, and iteration — ensuring the best user experience and business impact.

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary based on complexity. A basic website may take 2–4 weeks, while full-scale apps or platforms could take a few months. We'll provide a clear timeline before we begin.

Do you offer post-launch support and maintenance?

Yes! We offer post-launch support, bug fixing, and maintenance plans to ensure your project runs smoothly.

How do I get started with Ara Discover Technologies?

Just contact us through our website, and we’ll schedule a free consultation to understand your goals and propose the best solution.
